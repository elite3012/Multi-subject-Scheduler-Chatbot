Usage Guide

1. Managing Subjects

Add a Subject

add subject "[Name]" hours [Number] priority [HIGH|MEDIUM|LOW]

Example: add subject "Mathematics" hours 15 priority HIGH

Priority determines scheduling order. HIGH priority subjects get the best time slots.

List All Subjects

list subjects

Shows all subjects with their hours and priorities.

Remove a Subject

remove subject "[Name]"

Example: remove subject "Mathematics"

2. Setting Availability

Add Available Day

set availability on [YYYY-MM-DD] capacity [Number] hours

Example: set availability on 2025-12-25 capacity 8 hours

Capacity is the maximum study hours available on that day.

List Availability

list availability

Shows all dates and their study capacity.

3. Schedule Generation

Generate Schedule

generate schedule

Creates optimized study schedule based on subjects and availability.

Uses front-loading algorithm: distributes study hours across available days with higher allocation early on.

View Schedule

show schedule

Displays the generated schedule with daily breakdown.

4. Other Commands

Command History

show history

View all previously executed commands.

Clear All Data

clear all

Removes all subjects, availability, and schedule data.
